Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about St Peter's University

St. Peter's University offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and certificate programs across various disciplines including:
  • Faculty of Business (BBA, Diploma in Business Studies, Certificate in Entrepreneurship)
  • Faculty of Education (Bachelor of Education, Diploma in Primary Education, Certificate in Early Childhood Development)
  • Faculty of Information Technology (Bachelor of IT, Diploma in Computer Science, Certificate in Web Development)
  • Faculty of Health Sciences (Diploma in Nursing, Certificate in Health Assistance, Community Health)
  • Vocational Studies (Automotive, Electrical, Building & Construction, Fashion & Design, Catering & Hospitality)

You can apply for admission online through our official website. Visit the Admissions section above to find detailed instructions on the application process, required documents, and deadlines. Alternatively, you can contact our admissions office at admissions@spu.ac.ug or call 0704 343 621 for assistance.

SPU has two strategic campuses:
  • Luwero Main Campus: Located in the Ndejje school zone, Kisooba village - serene learning environment perfect for focused academic growth.
  • Kanyanya Campus: Situated about 4 miles from Kampala city centre along Gayaza Road (Behind Gaz fuel) - urban access for city residents.

You can contact the university through:
  • Phone: 0414 660 676, 0704 343 621, 0781 540 122
  • Email: info@spu.ac.ug (general inquiries) or admissions@spu.ac.ug (admission questions)
  • WhatsApp: 0704 343 621
  • Visit us: P.O. Box 16301, Kampala, Uganda

SPU provides comprehensive ICT services including:
  • Email management and communication tools
  • Hardware and software support
  • Campus-wide network and Wi-Fi access
  • IT security and data protection
  • E-Learning portal access at elearning.spu.ac.ug

Program durations vary by level:
  • Bachelor's degrees: Typically 3-4 years
  • Diploma programs: 2 years
  • Certificate programs: 1 year
  • Vocational courses: 6 months - 1 year
Specific durations are detailed in the program descriptions on our website.

SPU offers comprehensive student support services including:
  • Academic advising and mentorship
  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Mental health and wellness support
  • Disability support services
  • Student clubs and extracurricular activities
  • Library and research assistance
